The Best Christmas Movies According to Niki

My top 3 Christmas movies of all time.

Klaus

Heartwarming and silly. Love the animation style and how the mysteries of Santa Claus's magic are explained.

The Holiday

A story of perseverance and taking back control from those who mistreat us to find contentment and happiness with ourselves and those who appreciate us.

Mickey's Christmas Carol

Nostalgia definitely dictates this choice. It reminds me of the Christmases of my childhood in all the best ways and has always been my favorite version of the classic tale.
